# MATRIZ DE CRIATIVOS

> Para gerar prompts, combinar 1 valor de cada coluna
> Nunca repetir a mesma combinação em sequência
> Sempre variar: template, copy, fundo, cor, badge, CTA, confetti

## Templates (2)
- Shopping Bag
- Gift Box

## Copies (5)
- COPY 01: CHECK THIS | "Check out this" | "Find out what is inside. Conditions apply." | SEE DETAILS ->
- COPY 02: LOOK NOW | "Take a look at this" | "See what is included. Terms apply." | EXPLORE NOW ->
- COPY 03: DID YOU KNOW | "Look at this" | "Check the details inside. Conditions apply." | FIND OUT MORE ->
- COPY 04: WAIT WHAT | "Discover this" | "See what is new. Terms apply." | SEE WHAT IS NEW ->
- COPY 05: NO WAY | "Explore this" | "Find out how it works. Conditions apply." | EXPLORE ->
