# Criativo 1 — Popup Card (Deal Alert)

## Formato
900x900px popup card com gradiente radial + emoji

## Estrutura
- BACKGROUND: soft radial gradient, center-left white → {cor}, edge {cor}
- WHITE POPUP CARD: 75% width, 50% height, border-radius 28px, soft drop shadow
- Line 1: {emoji} (~44px) + "{título}" bold black 38px uppercase
- Line 2: "{subtítulo}" medium gray 24px regular
- Line 3: button pill-shaped, background {cor}, text "{CTA}" white bold 19px
- Footer: "*This is strictly informational and educational content" ~13px, medium gray

## Exemplo
```
900x900px. BACKGROUND: soft radial gradient, center-left white → white (#FFFFFF), edge white. Dreamy out-of-focus blob gradient, no sharp edges. WHITE POPUP CARD centered over background. ```
```
